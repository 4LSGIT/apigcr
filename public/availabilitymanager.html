Weekly hours

Engine truth: overlapping rows for the same weekday union into one working window; no rows for a weekday = not bookable that day. Valid from/to bounds a row to a date range (e.g. a temporary schedule); empty = always.

Personal blocks (time off)

Carves the provider's booking availability for the exact interval. All times are firm-local wall time.

Firm closures

Blocks every provider. Shabbos / Yom Tov rows are regenerated monthly from Hebcal — their times are not editable (an edit would be overwritten on the next run); deactivate/reactivate is allowed and survives regeneration. Manual closures are fully editable.
